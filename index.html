About The Team

The Golden State Warriors are a San Francisco-based professional basketball team in the United States. They are part of the Western Conference Pacific Division in the National Basketball Association (NBA). Originally established in Philadelphia in 1946, the Warriors relocated to the San Francisco Bay Area in 1962, adopting the city's name. In 1971, they changed their name to Golden State. The team's home games are hosted at the Chase Center.
