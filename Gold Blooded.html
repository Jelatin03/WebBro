Slogan





"WE ARE ALL GOLD BLOODED"


Credits to: Call_Me_JD


In the last two years, the Warriors did not find themselves in the playoffs, as they failed to qualify in 2020 and 2021. However, their strong performance during the regular season earned them a third-place finish in the final regular season standings, securing their spot in the playoffs. With their playoff berth confirmed, Steve Kerr's team opted to introduce a fresh playoff campaign rather than reusing a previous slogan. The Warriors have partnered with the Bay Area streetwear brand Adapt, which is renowned for the term 'Gold Blooded.' This collaboration is a fitting connection between the Warriors and Adapt, as Evan Lessler's company draws inspiration from the culture, music, and sports scene in the Bay Area.

“Gold Blooded captures the love and passion Dub Nation has for this team,” explained Warriors chief marketing officer Jen Millet when the tie-up was announced."

“There is an incredibly strong bond between the global fan base and the players on our roster."

“Throughout the 2022 playoffs, Gold Blooded will celebrate that bond,” she added.
