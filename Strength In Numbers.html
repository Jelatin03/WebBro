Slogan

2017 NBA Playoffs



For the Warriors, "Strength in Numbers" isn't just a catchy slogan. It's become a way of life, and a way of winning. "The ones that work are the ones that really tie back to the locker room itself," Welts said. And it comes from the top down said Welts, who has spent decades in the NBA.


The Golden State Warriors illustrate that their slogan, 'Strength in Numbers,' goes beyond mere marketing jargon. It encapsulates their core cultural philosophy. As a collective, the team embraces various off-court rituals, such as regular team meals, carpooling, and even exclusive 'players-only' group chats, fostering an environment of candid and open communication. These practices are typically associated with tightly-knit high school teams rather than a professional basketball franchise.


Even when they faced defeat, the Warriors' culture stood out as players took responsibility for their errors and refrained from pointing fingers, as exemplified by Draymond Green, who acknowledged his suspension's impact on their championship loss. The triumph of 'Strength in Numbers' has proven that consciously shaping and implementing a culture is just as relevant in the realm of sports.
